Palm Kernel Cake (PKC) Supply

We specialize in the production and supply of high-quality Palm Kernel Cake (PKC), a valuable byproduct of palm kernel oil extraction. PKC is rich in essential proteins and nutrients, making it an excellent addition to animal feed. It plays a crucial role in promoting healthy growth and development in livestock, including poultry, cattle, and other animals.

Our PKC is carefully processed to maintain its nutritional value, ensuring a premium product that supports the health and productivity of livestock. We adhere to strict quality control measures to provide a consistent and reliable supply in bulk or retail quantities. Partnering with farmers and agribusinesses, we cater to diverse market demands, making our PKC a trusted choice for the animal feed industry.

Slough Processing & Supply

Slough is a fibrous byproduct of palm kernel processing that has multiple sustainable uses, including biomass fuel and organic compost. We ensure that our slough is processed and supplied in a way that promotes eco-friendly practices. Industries looking for renewable energy sources use slough as a cost-effective biomass fuel, while farmers utilize it to improve soil fertility.

Our commitment to sustainability means that every part of the palm kernel is put to good use. By supplying slough, we contribute to reducing waste and promoting greener alternatives in both the energy and agricultural sectors.
