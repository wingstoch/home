About Us

At Wingstoch Agro Allied Ltd Palm Kernel Processing Ltd, we are committed to producing high-quality palm kernel products, including Palm Kernel Oil (PKO), Palm Kernel Cake (PKC), and Slough.
Located in the heart of Enugu, Nigeria, we leverage advanced processing techniques to ensure efficiency and sustainability.
