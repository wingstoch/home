About Us

Founded with a vision to revolutionize the palm kernel processing industry, WT Palm Kernel Processing Ltd. is a leading producer of high-quality palm kernel products. We specialize in the extraction of Palm Kernel Oil (PKO), a versatile ingredient used in cooking, cosmetics, and industrial applications. Additionally, we produce Palm Kernel Cake (PKC), a valuable livestock feed supplement, and Slough, an eco-friendly byproduct used as biomass fuel and organic compost.

With our state-of-the-art processing facility located at Segem, along Enugu Abakaliki road Eke Obinagu Emene, Enugu State, we ensure that every product meets high industry standards. Our team of experts is dedicated to maintaining quality, sustainability, and efficiency at every stage of production. By implementing modern processing techniques, we minimize waste and maximize output, making us a trusted supplier in the agricultural and industrial sectors.
